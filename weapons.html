Frag Grenade

The Frag Grenade is a handheld explosive weapon that is thrown at the enemy, and is first acquired in the level The UAC. It is an equipment item, and recharges after use. -- DOOM Fandom (link)

Burst Rifle

The Burst Rifle can be unlocked in the multiplayer at level 3. The player spawns with 60 ammo (90 is the max ammo). The weapon features two fire modes: Primary Fire - A selective-fire battle rifle equipped with reciprocating barrel assembly and 3-round burst mode. Each burst does a total of 30 damage, with 10 damage dealt per round. Weapon Mod - Aims down the sights and selects a semi-automatic mode using magnum ammunition (Hold the Weapon Mod button). Damage is slightly increased to 16. -- DOOM Fandom (link)
